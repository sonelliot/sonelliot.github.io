Education

I majored in *Games Programming* and *Software Engineering* at Edith Cowan University (2006 - 2009) and graduated with distinction. While I was there I worked on some interesting projects, including:

a mobile puzzle game

I wrote a small puzzle game for the original iPhone using `OpenGLES` and `Objective-C`. The objective of the game was to mix coloured blobs together to form a target colour. I used [metaballs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metaballs) to create a lava lamp like effect for the game. The nature of this algorithm made it difficult to achieve a good framerate on the physical device, so I spent a long time optimizing the code for better performance.

an autonomous submarine simulation

For my final year project I worked with a post-grad student from [UWA](http://www.uwa.edu.au) to make improvements to an existing submarine simulation developed by the University. My task was to integrate a CUDA-powered particle simulation, that I had developed in a previous unit, into the existing codebase to create more realistic water acting on the submarine body.

In the end we could not simulate enough particles in realtime to maintain a stable simulation. It was an interesting project and the outcome may have been different with today's hardware.
